## The Business Case for Cloud

The ROI of cloud — when done right — extends far beyond infrastructure cost savings:

But the real business case goes beyond these numbers:

- **Agility** — Cloud-native organizations can respond to market changes in days, not quarters. New features, new markets, new products — all become faster.
- **Scalability** — Handle 10x traffic spikes without over-provisioning. Pay for what you use.
- **Innovation Velocity** — Access to managed AI/ML services, data analytics platforms, and cutting-edge tools that would take years to build on-premise.
- **Talent Attraction** — Top engineers want to work with modern cloud-native stacks, not legacy on-premise infrastructure.
